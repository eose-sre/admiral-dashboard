SERLF Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

OUR COMMITMENT: Your data sovereignty is sacred. We collect only what's necessary to operate the service and never access your AI silos without explicit consent.

1. Overview

SERLF is built on the principle of data sovereignty. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, what we absolutely do NOT collect, and how we protect your privacy.

2. What We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary for service operation:

  • Account Information: Email address for authentication and service communications
  • Usage Metrics: Service performance data, feature usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Technical Logs: System performance, error logs, security events (no customer content)
  • Billing Information: Payment processing data (processed by certified third parties)

What We Absolutely DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ Your AI conversations or interactions
  • ❌ Data from your sovereign AI silos
  • ❌ Your browsing history or personal behavior
  • ❌ Content of your AI models or training data
  • ❌ Customer business data or documents
  • ❌ Location data or device identifiers
  • ❌ Social media or external account information

3. Data Sovereignty Architecture

SERLF operates under a strict sovereignty model:

  • Your data never leaves your designated geographic region without consent
  • All customer silos are isolated and customer-controlled
  • We cannot access your AI infrastructure without explicit written permission
  • Cross-border data transfers require customer approval

4. Compliance Framework

  • PIPEDA (Canada): Full compliance with minimal collection and explicit consent
  • GDPR (EU): Complete compliance including data portability and erasure rights
  • CCPA (California): Full compliance, no sale of personal data

5. Data Residency and Location

  • Default Location: Canada (Toronto region)
  • Customer Choice: You select data residency location
  • Available Regions: Canada, EU, US (with customer consent)
  • Data Processing: Only within your selected region

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while account is active
  • Usage Metrics: Anonymized data retained for 2 years maximum
  • Customer Content: We don't have access, so nothing to retain
  • Upon Termination: Account deletion within 30 days, all data purged

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 Universal Rights

  • Access: Request information about data we hold
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information
  • Deletion: Complete account and data deletion
  • Portability: Export your data in standard formats

7.2 Regional Rights

  • EU Residents: Full GDPR rights including data protection officer contact
  • California Residents: CCPA rights including opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data anyway)
  • Canadian Residents: PIPEDA rights and complaint process

8. Data Security

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Zero-trust security architecture
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Incident response procedures with customer notification
  • Staff security training and background checks

9. Third-Party Services

  • Payment Processing: PCI DSS compliant processors only
  • Email Delivery: Transactional emails only
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data only

All third parties sign data processing agreements.

10. Cookies and Tracking

  • Essential Cookies: Authentication and security only
  • No Tracking: No advertising cookies or behavioral tracking
  • No Analytics Tracking: No Google Analytics or similar tools
  • Customer Control: Cookie preferences in account settings

11. International Transfers

Customer data is processed only in customer-selected regions. Any international transfers require:

  • Explicit customer consent
  • Adequate protection mechanisms
  • Documentation of transfer purposes
  • Customer right to revoke consent

12. Children's Privacy

SERLF is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

13. Policy Updates

We may update this policy occasionally. Material changes will be communicated via email with 30 days' notice. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

14. Data Breach Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach:

  • Immediate containment and investigation
  • Customer notification within 72 hours
  • Regulatory authority notification as required
  • Detailed incident report and remediation plan
